The game consists of two levels. You can control the player using the keyboard arrows (up, down, left, right) or the WASD keys. Additional commands will be communicated during gameplay.
Pressing the up and down arrow keys moves the player forward or backward, while using the left and right arrow keys rotates the player in place.
The first level is a maze that must be navigated under poor lighting conditions, requiring the use of a light torch to illuminate the way. Once you have found the generator and placed it on the platform, the light will be restored, and the door to the next level will open.
In the second level, you will be tasked with finding the correct color cube combination under non-white light. Pay attention: if you make a mistake while selecting the cubes, you will need to restart the sequence from the first cube.
